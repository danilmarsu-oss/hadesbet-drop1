Understanding Problem Gambling

Problem gambling can affect anyone, regardless of age, background, or experience. It is important to recognise the warning signs early so that appropriate action can be taken. At HadesBet Casino, we encourage all of our players to be honest with themselves about their gambling behaviour. Some common signs of problem gambling include:

  • Spending more money on gambling than you can afford to lose
  • Chasing losses in an attempt to win back money
  • Neglecting personal, professional, or family responsibilities due to gambling
  • Feeling anxious, irritable, or restless when not gambling
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gambling activity
  • Lying to friends or family about the extent of your gambling
  • Using gambling as a way to escape stress, anxiety, or other personal problems

If you recognise any of these signs in yourself or someone you know, we strongly encourage you to seek help immediately. You are not alone, and there are many organisations and resources available to assist you.
